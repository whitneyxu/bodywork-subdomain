assisted stretch therapy

30 minutes assisted stretch therapy

~ $30 - $45

My approach is goal-oriented, collaborative, and emergent - guided by your goals. Through trial and discussion, we will find the plan that works best for you. These sessions will focus on deep, intentional breathing as I move you through a series of tailored stretches to address your needs.

I recommend this option for people who have a specific short-term need that they would like to address, maybe your hips are tight, maybe you notice your shoulders are tense, or you are recovering from an injury. This is also a good option for people who are curious if stretch therapy could be helpful for them.

*Please wear non-restrictive clothes that you feel comfortable moving in.

Benefits of stretching

  • Increased bodily awareness - allowing yourself to tap into and be present with your body through assisted movement
  • Improved breath work
  • Increased flexibility and fluidity of joint movement
  • Improved activity performance
  • Decreased chronic or short term joint and muscle pain
  • Improved sleep and relaxation
