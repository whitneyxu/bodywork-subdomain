Diaphragmatic breathing Do you know the correct way of breathing? It's not the "belly breaths" that you may have learned in yoga. Diaphragmatic breathing utilizes your diaphragm, or breathing muscle, ensuring that you get the full intake of air into your lungs. Improper breathing technique can lead to shallow breathing, a tight diaphragm, and a stressed out nervous system. This video teaches you step-by-step how to breathe with your diaphragm. YouTube Video
